About

This is the official website of Artist Aadii. His real name is Adhil K N. He was born on 17th September, 1997 at Kaipamangalam, Thrissur district. He is an artist who is very keen in learning and teaching art skills on a continuous basis. His motto is to build up a group of dedicated artists by changing their perspective in such a way that help them flourish.

You can navigate to Artist Aadii's YouTube tutorial videos from here . He takes classes through YouTube and on online basis through with you can upgrade your skill & talent; if you are serious about your art then you can ask your queries, doubts with our team who are happy to help. Hoping that this website will enable you to improve your skills and thereby making Adhil's even more effort worthy.

He does freelancing jobs on graphic designing, film production, poster designing, animation , portraits, caricature etc; which will be properly delivered on time.

Currently he is persuing his bachelor degree on digital Film production in MES Asmabi College, Vemballur. Mediums used by Artist Aadii : Graphite pencils Acrylic painting Digital art Wall art
